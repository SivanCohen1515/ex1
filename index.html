Mushroom cream pasta

There’s few things in this world that can rival the feeling of satisfying a craving
for a big bowl of creamy pasta. And this one not only hits the spot, it’s so good it’s
going to have you doing the happy dance around the kitchen (because there’s every
possibility that half of it never makes your plate!).

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of pasta
  • 1 container of cooking cream
  • Olive oil
  • 2 cloves crushed garlic
  • Salt
  • Nutmeg

Preparation steps:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the preparation instructions.
  2. In a pan, fry in a little olive oil or butter, crushed garlic.
  3. Add cooking cream and heat over low heat until boiling.
  4. Add salt and a little nutmeg.
  5. Pour the sauce over the chilled pasta, before serving.
